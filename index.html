RoadFix - Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

1. Introduction

RoadFix (“we”, “our”, “us”) provides a mobile application that allows users to report road and infrastructure issues. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

We collect the following personal information:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Account profile details
  • Device ID

2.2 Location Information

We may collect approximate or precise location when you report an issue or use location-based features.

2.3 Photos & Media

You may upload photos to report road issues. These are stored securely.

2.4 Log & Usage Data

We automatically collect:

  • Device information (model, OS version)
  • App activity and actions performed
  • Error logs and crash reports

2.5 Authentication Data

We use Firebase Authentication (email + password) to manage user accounts.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account
  • To allow you to submit road issues
  • To improve app performance
  • To communicate with you
  • For security and fraud prevention

4. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your data. We may share your information with:

  • Google Firebase services
  • Google Maps APIs
  • Government or municipal authorities (only for reported issues)
  • Legal authorities when required

5. Data Security

Your data is stored using secure industry-standard technologies including Firebase Authentication, Firestore, and Firebase Storage.

6. Data Retention

We keep your data for as long as necessary to provide services. You may request deletion of your account at any time.

7. Children's Privacy

RoadFix is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Your Rights

  • Request access to your data
  • Request correction or deletion
  • Withdraw consent

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Continued use of the app means you accept the updated policy.
